Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, process, and disclose your information, including personal information, in conjunction with your access to and use of our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register for our services, including:

  • Personal Details: Full Name, Email Address, Phone Number, Age, Gender, Location, Profession, Race, Faith, Height, and information about children.
  • Matching Preferences: Details about your ideal partner, including age range, location, race, faith, and core values.
  • Profile Information: Your personal descriptions, vision for marriage, and what family means to you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide and manage our matchmaking services. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • To create and maintain your user profile.
  • To identify and introduce you to potential matches based on your preferences.
  • To communicate with you regarding our services and your matches.
  • To improve and personalize your experience on our website.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We will only share your information with a potential match after both parties have given explicit consent for an introduction. We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights or property.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. We use secure servers and employ technical and organizational measures to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of your data.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
